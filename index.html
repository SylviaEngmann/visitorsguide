Computer Science Department

This is one of the three major departments in Ashesi

Ashesi's Computer Science programme focuses not only on software design and programming, but also on empowering young people to be at the forefront of African growth by harnessing the power of technology. Students in the Computer Science programme are taken through robust foundational courses that prepare them to engage new technologies, and create software tools for African needs.The offices of the department are housed in the King Engineering building.

Engineering Department

This is one of the three major departments in Ashesi

The goals of Ashesi's Engineering Programme is to give students broad engineering skills that will enable them solve the challenging problems of our society, and core entrepreneurial skills to think about how to transfer their solutions to the marketplace.

Todd and Ruth Warren Library

The Todd and Ruth Warren Library, one of the biggest landmarks on the Ashesi Campus, is home to over 1500 books, electronincs and physical, to aid in research, or just for pure reading pleasure. It also contains spaces for group meetings, studying spaces and has printers and photocopiers for use.
